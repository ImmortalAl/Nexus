2-3 minutes
Usage: Use at the very beginning of your broadcast to set the tone and establish energy.

"Welcome back, truth seekers. I'm [Your Name], and you're tuned in to [Show Name]."

"Tonight, we're diving deep into the stories they don't want you to hear. The conversations they're trying to silence."

*(Pause, let energy build)*

*(Powerful music, quick fade)*

"And before we're done tonight... I'm going to show you something that proves there's still hope."
