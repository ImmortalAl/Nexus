Pigeon Icon Designs - Round 2

Proper pigeon anatomy: plump body, tiny head, stubby beak

Pigeon Anatomy Rules:

  • Body is 80% of the bird - Pigeons are CHUBBY. The body dominates.
  • Head is tiny - About 1/5 the body size, sits on short neck
  • Beak is short and stubby - Not pointed like eagles or long like herons
  • Wings are rounded - Curved tips, not angular/swept like raptors
  • Overall shape: pear/teardrop - Heavy bottom, small top
